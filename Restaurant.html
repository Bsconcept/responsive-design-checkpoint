About Us

Our Story

Since 2010, Le Bistro has been serving exceptional cuisine in the heart of the city.

Founded by Chef Michael Thompson, a Culinary Institute of America graduate with over 20 years of experience in Michelin-starred restaurants, Le Bistro brings together traditional French techniques with modern culinary innovation.

We believe in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that not only satisfy your hunger but also tell a story. Our commitment to quality and sustainability has made us a favorite among food enthusiasts and critics alike.

Whether you're joining us for a romantic dinner, a business lunch, or a family celebration, we promise an unforgettable dining experience.
